Accessible Rock Band

This is a project that I created for Machine Learning for the Arts, a class I took. I created this because I am interested in accessibility and how machine learning can really help in this field. This is a game that can be played on p5 just by moving your nose from side to side to hit notes, similar to something like rock band or guitar hero. I made this accessible by making it so that anyone that can turn their head just a little to each side is able to play this. Here is a demonstration, and here is how you can play it yourself.
